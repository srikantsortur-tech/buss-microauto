Future Outlook & Recommendations

The market is poised for continued change. Based on current trends and challenges, this section outlines key projections for the next five years and provides actionable recommendations for cities and operators.

Key Projections (2025-2030)

  • Market to double to $70B by 2030.
  • E-bike segment to grow fastest (12% CAGR).
  • Increased integration with public transport apps.
  • Battery-swapping tech to become standard.
  • Stricter regulations on fleet sizes and parking.

Recommendations

  • Cities: Invest in dedicated, protected micromobility lanes.
  • Operators: Prioritize vehicle durability and safety features.
  • All: Standardize data-sharing protocols for urban planning.
  • Cities: Implement "slow zones" in pedestrian-heavy areas.
  • Operators: Offer more subscription and long-term rental plans.
